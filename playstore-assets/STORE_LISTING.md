### Learn Piano the Fun Way with Pianio! 🎹

Pianio is an interactive piano learning app that makes mastering the piano enjoyable for learners of all ages. Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to improve your skills, Pianio offers personalized lessons, real-time feedback, and engaging practice exercises.

### 🎯 Key Features

**Interactive Lessons**
- Step-by-step tutorials with falling note visualization
- Hand placement guides with finger numbering
- Real-time audio feedback using your device's microphone
- MIDI keyboard support for precise practice

**Personalized Learning**
- Age-appropriate content for kids (5-8, 9-12), teens, and adults
- Skill level assessment (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
- Customizable learning goals (fun, classical, pop, jazz, exams)
- Adaptive difficulty based on your progress

**Extensive Song Library**
- Built-in curriculum with popular songs
- Classical masterpieces from public domain sources
- Holiday favorites and contemporary hits
- New songs added regularly

**Progress Tracking**
- XP and leveling system
- Achievement badges and rewards
- Practice streaks and daily goals
- Detailed performance analytics

**Practice Tools**
- Adjustable tempo and animation speed
- Dark mode for comfortable practice
- Multiple input modes (MIDI, microphone, touch)
- Metronome and rhythm exercises

### 🎓 For Different Learners

**Kids (5-8 years)**
- Large, colorful notes and animations
- Short, engaging lessons
- Fun rewards and achievements
- Parent-friendly progress tracking

**Teens (9-12 years)**
- Challenging quests and missions
- Popular song selections
- Social features and competitions
- Skill-building exercises

**Teens (13-17 years)**
- Modern songs and genres
- Advanced techniques
- Performance preparation
- Creative expression tools

**Adults (18+ years)**
- Comprehensive music theory
- Classical repertoire
- Professional techniques
- Flexible scheduling

### 🎹 What You'll Learn

- Note reading and music theory
- Proper hand positioning and finger technique
- Rhythm and timing
- Scales and chords
- Song interpretation and expression
- Practice habits and discipline

### 🔧 Technical Features

- **WebXR Support**: Practice in virtual reality for immersive learning
- **Offline Mode**: Download lessons for practice without internet
- **Cross-Platform**: Use on phones, tablets, and desktops
- **Cloud Sync**: Save progress across devices (optional)
- **Multiple Languages**: English, Chinese, Japanese, German, Spanish
